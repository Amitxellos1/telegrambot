# Technical FAQ

## Password Reset

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Visit the IT portal at it.company.com/reset and enter your employee email. A reset link will be sent within 5 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact IT support at it-support@company.com.

## VPN Access

**Q: How do I connect to the company VPN?**
A: Download the company VPN client from the IT portal. Use your employee credentials to log in. The VPN is required for accessing internal systems when working remotely. Contact IT if you need your VPN credentials reset.

## Software Installation

**Q: Can I install software on my work computer?**
A: Only approved software from the company software center can be installed. To request new software, submit a ticket through the IT portal with business justification. Installation of unauthorized software may result in security violations.

## Email Setup

**Q: How do I set up company email on my phone?**
A: Go to Settings > Mail > Add Account > Microsoft Exchange. Enter your company email and password. The server address is mail.company.com. Enable two-factor authentication for mobile email access.

## Printer Issues

**Q: The printer isn't working, what should I do?**
A: First, check if the printer is turned on and has paper. Try restarting both the printer and your computer. If the issue persists, submit a ticket to IT support with the printer location and error message.

## Data Backup

**Q: How is my data backed up?**
A: All files stored in the company cloud drive are automatically backed up daily. Local files on your computer are backed up weekly. For critical projects, we recommend saving to the cloud drive. Recovery requests can be made through IT support.
