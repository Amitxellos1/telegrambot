# Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy

Employees are eligible for remote work after completing their 3-month probation period. Remote work requests must be submitted to the HR department at least one week in advance. Employees working remotely are expected to be available during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM) and must have a stable internet connection.

## Leave Policy

- **Annual Leave**: All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
- **Sick Leave**: Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- **Parental Leave**: New parents are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence. All expenses require receipts and manager approval. The company reimburses travel expenses at standard mileage rates. International travel requires pre-approval from department heads.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior in the workplace. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind is strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR immediately and will be investigated thoroughly.

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday through Friday. Overtime must be pre-approved by managers. Employees working overtime are entitled to time-off-in-lieu or overtime pay as per local regulations.
