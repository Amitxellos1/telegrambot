# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to the Team!

Congratulations on joining our company! This guide will help you get started during your first week.

## Day 1 Checklist

- [ ] Collect your employee badge from reception
- [ ] Set up your workstation with IT support
- [ ] Complete HR paperwork and sign employment documents
- [ ] Attend the welcome orientation at 10 AM in Conference Room A
- [ ] Meet your team and manager
- [ ] Set up your email and Slack accounts

## First Week Tasks

1. **Complete Training Modules**: Access the learning portal at learn.company.com and complete all mandatory training within your first week.
2. **Review Documentation**: Read through department documentation and project wikis.
3. **Schedule 1-on-1s**: Book introductory meetings with key team members.
4. **Set Up Development Environment**: Work with your tech lead to configure your development tools.

## Important Contacts

- **HR Department**: hr@company.com | Extension 100
- **IT Support**: it-support@company.com | Extension 200
- **Facilities**: facilities@company.com | Extension 300
- **Your Manager**: Check your welcome email for contact details

## Office Facilities

- **Cafeteria**: 8th floor, open 8 AM - 6 PM
- **Gym**: Basement level, 24/7 access with badge
- **Parking**: Underground parking available, register your vehicle with Facilities
- **Meeting Rooms**: Book via the calendar app, max 2 hours per booking

## Probation Period

Your first 3 months are a probation period. During this time:
- You'll have weekly check-ins with your manager
- Complete all mandatory training
- Receive feedback on your performance
- Full benefits activate after successful completion

## Questions?

Don't hesitate to ask! Your team and HR are here to help you succeed. You can also check the employee handbook at handbook.company.com for detailed policies.
